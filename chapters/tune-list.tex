\chapter*{List of Recordings}

\blankfootnote{Jazz discography in the 21st century can be frustrating, given
  the many different formats and reissues of original sources. I have tried to
  be as clear as possible here, listing both original release information and
  more readily available reissues, when available. Three online sources have
  been invaluable in compiling this information: The Jazz Discography
  Project (\url{http://www.jazzdisco.org}; Nobuaki Togashi, Kohji ``Shaolin''
  Matsubayashi, and Masayuki Hatta, maintainers); Discogs
  (\url{http://www.discogs.com}; created by Kevin Lewandowski); and AllMusic
  (\url{http://www.allmusic.com}; All Media Network).}%
%
This appendix provides a list of all of the musical examples used in this
dissertation, along with recordings where they can be found. For each tune, I
have tried to provide at least two reference recordings; they have been
chosen because they present clear statements of the standard changes analyzed
in the text. Recordings are listed here only by the artist and album title;
full details can be found in the discography that follows. In cases where a
tune has a recording that is considered definitive, it is marked with a star.
